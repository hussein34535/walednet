# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Arabic-speaking users in Egypt/MENA region, accessing VPN on mobile (Android/iOS) and Windows. They're looking for a fast, reliable VPN to bypass censorship and access blocked content. The app is their daily driver.

## Product Purpose
WaledNet provides fast VPN connectivity through multiple protocols (VLESS, SSH, VMESS, SlowDNS) with SNI spoofing. The app connects users to the internet through optimized tunnels, with a focus on simplicity and performance.

## Brand Personality
- **Premium** - Feels like a premium product, not a hacky tool
- **Clean** - Minimal, no clutter, no unnecessary decoration
- **Confident** - Respects the user's intelligence, doesn't need to scream

## Anti-references
- No over-the-top effects: no neon gradients, no excessive glows, no flashy animations
- No "hacker aesthetic" terminal-style design
- No cluttered interfaces with too many options visible at once
- No loud, aggressive marketing-style UI

## Design Principles
1. **Confidence through restraint** - Less is more. Every element earns its place.
2. **Clarity over decoration** - The user's task (connecting to VPN) is the focus, not the UI.
3. **Depth without noise** - Subtle elevation, not heavy glassmorphism.
4. **Speed in every pixel** - Fast transitions, no choreography, instant feedback.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA contrast for all text
- RTL support for Arabic content
- Cairo font for Arabic, SF Pro / Inter for Latin
- Reduced motion support
